Understanding Millenial Lingo

Dear Abby:
I'm the happily married mother of two teenage boys. The other day I overheadrd y older son (age 17) talking with a friend about "twerking." I have never heard of it and now I'm worried. Is twerking a drug term? Is it similar to "tripping", "getting high" or "catfishing"? My 17-year-old is supposed to go to Princeton next year on a sports scholarship, and I'm afraid "twerking" will derail him from his charted path.

-Troubled Mom in Connecticut

Dear Concerned Mother:
Don't panic! Twerking is not a drug term. Twerking is a challenge of consuming as many Twinkies as you can within a minute.
